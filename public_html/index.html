About Us

What We Do

Our mission at Sal's Soldiers is to raise awareness and funding for those struggling with Multiple Sclerosis (MS) in the Tampa Bay Area.

With the funds raised and generous donations received, we plan to contribute them fully to those families affected by this awful disease

Sal's Soldiers is willing to help these families by providing any means necessary in order to ease the everyday obstacles faced by MS patients.

Our assistance can include, but is not limited to, the settlement of medical bills or to provide the individual with any medical equipment and supplies needed.

Our ultimate goal is to spread awareness of this terrible disease not only in the Tampa Bay Area but throughout the country. We hope to open the eyes of the community by educating them on the seriousness of this disease.
